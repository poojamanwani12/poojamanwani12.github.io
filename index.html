Research focus

  • PM2.5: chemistry, optics & episodic events

    Biomass burning, dust intrusions, festive fireworks; impacts on regional air quality.

  • Meteorology & transport

    Back-trajectory clusters, seasonal drivers, dispersion & potential source regions (HYSPLIT).

  • Aerosol acidity & health-relevant metrics

    Multi-proxy acidity, neutralization, oxidative potential; regional comparisons.

  • Source apportionment & ML

    EPA-PMF 5.0 + hybrid ML to isolate meteorology-driven variability.

  • Uncertainty & sensitivity

    Fuzzy logic + Monte Carlo for health-risk assessment of PM2.5 exposure.
