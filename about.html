About Me

I am a PhD scholar at IIT Bombay, studying aerosols and air quality to better understand their chemical composition and their health, climate, and policy impacts. My work brings together science, data, and communication with the goal of translating research into real-world environmental action.

Thank you for taking the time to visit and learn about my work!
